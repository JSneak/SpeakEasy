Think. Engage. Discuss.

With SpeakEasy, a culmination of these three key steps in the learning process is achieved. In an easy and organized manner, your classroom can become a discussion ground for literature, history, politics, art, and so on. Simply tap the button to be added to the speaking list and tap it again to be removed. SpeakEasy leaves no doubt as to who should be the next to contribute and overall creates a more stable and efficient classroom environment.
